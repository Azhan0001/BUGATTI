HERITAGE

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1909

1909

The Beginning

Ettore Bugatti founds the company in Molsheim, Alsace, beginning a legacy of automotive excellence.

1924

Type 35

The legendary Type 35 debuts, becoming the most successful racing car in history with over 2,000 victories.

1936

Type 57SC Atlantic

The iconic Atlantic is born, now considered one of the most valuable cars ever made.

2005

Veyron Era

The Veyron 16.4 launches, becoming the world's fastest production car at 407 km/h.

2016

Chiron Arrives

The Chiron succeeds the Veyron, pushing boundaries with 1,500 HP and revolutionary engineering.

2024

The Future

Bugatti continues to redefine automotive excellence, blending heritage with cutting-edge innovation.
